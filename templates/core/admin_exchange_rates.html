Exchange Rate Impact on CNF Prices

The exchange rate between USD and SDG has a significant impact on CNF price calculations:

  • Direct Conversion Impact

    All local costs (in SDG) are converted to USD using the exchange rate.

  • Price Competitiveness

    Changes in exchange rates directly affect the international competitiveness of products.

  • Historical Tracking

    The system maintains a history of all exchange rates to track changes over time.

Best Practice: Update exchange rates at least weekly, or immediately after significant market changes.
